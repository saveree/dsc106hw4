Shahrukh

by Saveree Joshipura

"Very few people can turn their passion into profession. I am one of those lucky few..."

Shahrukh Khan is a famous film actor, television host, and film producer in India. Due to his immense popularity, he is frequently dubbed as the 'King of Bollywood' or 'King Khan.' Shahrukh Khan has acted in more than 85 Hindi films. He is known for being one of the most successful stars in the world. With 3.2 billion fans around the world, title of third richest actor in the world, an honorary award from the government of India (called the Padma Shri), an honorary pHd, and much more accolade, SRK remains my personal favorite!
